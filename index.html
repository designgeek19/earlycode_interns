Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internship program

What is the duration of the internship program?
Our internship program typically runs for 3-6 months, with flexible start dates throughout the year. We offer both full-time and part-time options to accommodate academic schedules.
Is this a paid internship?
This is not a paid internship. However, we provide our interns with hands-on training, mentorship, and career development support to prepare them for job opportunities.
What technical skills do I need to apply?
You don’t need any prior technical skills to apply. Our internship program is designed to accommodate beginners as well as those with some experience. We provide the training and guidance you need to become proficient in your chosen area during the program.
Can I apply if I'm a beginner with little experience?
Absolutely! We have positions suitable for beginners. We're primarily looking for enthusiasm, willingness to learn, and problem-solving aptitude rather than extensive experience.
What kind of projects will I work on?
You'll work on real projects that impact businesses or clients. This could include web development, mobile app development, data analysis, UX/UI design, or other IT-related tasks depending on your role.
Will I receive mentorship during the internship?
Yes, each intern is paired with an experienced mentor who provides guidance, feedback, and support throughout the program.
Are remote internships available?
We do not offer remote internships. All our internship programs are held on-site to ensure effective hands-on training, teamwork, and mentorship.
What is the application process?
Our process typically includes: 1) SIWES application and acceptance, 2) Course registration and enrollment, 3) Onboarding with mentors, and 4) Training and mentorship for successful candidates.
Is there a possibility of full-time employment after the internship?
There is no guarantee of full-time employment after the internship. However, top-performing interns may be considered for job openings based on their performance and the company's needs.
